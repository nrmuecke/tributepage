Elminster Aumar

The Sage of Shadowdale

Here are a few of Elminster's accomplishments:

  • Recognized as one of the most famous and powerful wizards in all of Faerun
  • Slew an elder orb beholder
  • Took part in re-founding the Harpers
  • One of Mystra's chosen and also a weave anchor
  • Writer of multiple books
  • Had a drink named after him "Elminster's Choice"
